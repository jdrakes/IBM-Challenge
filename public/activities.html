Camp Activities

List of all camp activities

Ages Indoor/Outdoor Activity Name Description
All Indoor Space Station Mission Campers will embark on a simulated mission and have to use teamwork to solve problems and get through necessary tasks to complete their mission in our mock-up space station.
All Indoor Mars Mission Similar to the Space Station Mission campers will work through simulated problems and have to work as a team to complete tasks in our mock-up Mars surface arena.
All Indoor History of Space Exploration Campers see an interactive and fun presentation of the history of space exploration.
All Indoor/Outdoor Experiments Campers will conduct various scientific experiments appropriate for their ages.
All Inddor IMAXÂ® Movies Campers will watch a space themed film appropriate for their age group in the IMAX Spacedome theater.
All Indoor Astronaut Simulators Campers get to experience that 1/6th gravity chair simulator and the multi-axis trainer which give them an idea of what it would feel like to be working in space.
All Outdoor Rocket Construction and Launch Campers will work together to build a model rocket and go through the launching process.
All Outdoor Tour a Space Shuttle Campers will be taken through a real space shuttle to see what the real thing looks like.
13+ Outdoor Communication Activities in the Underwater Astronaut Trainer Campers can experience the underwater astronaut trainer that is used to train real astronauts, they will participate in some simple communication activities in the trainer.
15+ Indoor Scuba Space Walk Training Campers can spend time going through the same space walk training that beginner astronauts go through.
15+ Indoor Aeronautic Design Lecture Campers will attend a lecture from one of our top aeronautic engineers on the basics of aeronautic design.
15+ Indoor Space Suit Design Campers will learn about how space suits are designed to best suit astronauts working in space and ensure their safety.
9+ Inddor Outing to Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum Campers will go on an educational outing to the National Air and Space Museum and engage in all of the activities and learning opportunities at the museum. *the camp covers the fee for the museum.
9+ Outdoor Stargazing Campers will experience stargazing in a new way - with some of our best telescopes. Campers will be able to explore space and learn more about planets and constellations while seeing them for themselves.
All Indoor Astronaut Dinner Campers will experience a dinner together, where they will eat meals that astronauts in space eat everyday.
